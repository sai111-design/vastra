# Return & Exchange Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026

At Vastra, we want you to love what you wear. If something doesn't work out, we're here to help.

---

## Returns

We accept returns within **7 days** of delivery, subject to the following conditions:

### Eligibility
- Items must be **unworn, unwashed, and undamaged**, with all original tags attached.
- Items must be returned in the **original packaging** (polybag or box).
- Footwear must be tried on indoors only — scuffed soles will not be accepted.

### Non-Returnable Items
- **Sale or clearance items** are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.
- **Innerwear, socks, and accessories** (belts, bags, hats) are non-returnable for hygiene reasons.
- Items purchased during promotional events (e.g., flash sales) are final sale unless stated otherwise.
- Items without original tags or packaging.

### How to Initiate a Return
1. Chat with our shopping assistant or email **returns@vastra.shop** within 7 days of delivery.
2. Provide your order number and reason for return.
3. We will arrange a **reverse pickup** at no additional cost, or you may ship the item back using any tracked courier.
4. Once we receive and inspect the item, we will process your refund.

---

## Exchanges

We offer **one free exchange per order** for a different size or colour of the same item, subject to availability.

- Exchange requests must be made within **7 days** of delivery.
- The replacement item will be shipped within **3–5 business days** after we receive the original item.
- If the requested size or colour is out of stock, we will issue a full refund instead.

---

## Refunds

- Refunds are processed to the **original payment method** within **10 business days** of receiving the returned item.
- **Cash on Delivery (COD) orders** will be refunded via bank transfer — you will need to provide your bank account details.
- Shipping charges (if any were paid) are **non-refundable** unless the return is due to a defect or wrong item.

---

## Damaged or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item:
- Contact us within **48 hours** of delivery with photos of the item and packaging.
- We will arrange an immediate replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

---

## Contact

- **Chat:** Use the Vastra shopping assistant on our website
- **Email:** returns@vastra.shop
- **Response Time:** Within 24 hours on business days
