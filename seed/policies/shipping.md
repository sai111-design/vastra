# Shipping Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026

We deliver across India. Here's everything you need to know about getting your order to your door.

---

## Shipping Options

| Option | Delivery Time | Cost |
|--------|--------------|------|
| **Standard Delivery** | 5–7 business days | Free on orders above ₹999; ₹79 for orders below ₹999 |
| **Express Delivery** | 2–3 business days | ₹149 (flat rate, all orders) |

- Business days are Monday through Saturday, excluding national holidays.
- Delivery times are estimated from the date of dispatch, not the date of order.

---

## Free Shipping

- **Orders above ₹999** qualify for free standard shipping automatically — no coupon needed.
- Free shipping applies to standard delivery only. Express delivery is always ₹149.
- Free shipping is calculated on the order subtotal before any discounts or coupons.

---

## Order Processing

- Orders placed **before 2:00 PM IST** on a business day are dispatched the same day.
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on Sundays/holidays are dispatched the next business day.
- You will receive a **tracking number via email and SMS** once your order is dispatched.

---

## Cash on Delivery (COD)

- Cash on delivery is available for orders with a subtotal **under ₹5,000**.
- A COD handling fee of **₹29** is added at checkout.
- COD is not available for express delivery.
- Please keep the exact amount ready — our delivery partners may not carry change.

---

## Delivery Coverage

- We deliver to all major cities and most pin codes across India via our logistics partners.
- Some remote or restricted pin codes may not be serviceable. Enter your pin code at checkout to verify.
- **P.O. Box addresses** are not supported.

---

## Order Tracking

- Track your order anytime using the tracking link sent via email/SMS.
- You can also ask our shopping assistant: *"Where is my order?"* with your order number.

---

## Delivery Issues

| Issue | What to Do |
|-------|-----------|
| **Not delivered within estimated time** | Contact us — we'll check with our logistics partner and update you. |
| **Damaged package on arrival** | Refuse delivery if visibly damaged, or contact us within 48 hours with photos. |
| **Wrong address provided** | Contact us before dispatch to update. After dispatch, return-to-origin charges may apply. |
| **Undeliverable / returned to origin** | We will contact you to reship or process a refund. |

---
