# Size Guide

Find your perfect fit using the measurements below. All measurements are in **inches**.

---

## How to Measure

- **Chest:** Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.
- **Waist:** Measure around your natural waistline, just above the navel.
- **Hip:** Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
- **Length (Top):** Measured from the highest point of the shoulder to the bottom hem.
- **Length (Bottom):** Measured from the waist seam along the outer leg to the hem (outseam).

## Fit Notes

- **Regular Fit:** True to size. Comfortable without being tight or loose.
- **Relaxed Fit:** Slightly roomier through the chest and body. Great for layering.
- **Oversized Fit:** Intentionally larger — dropped shoulders, boxy cut. If unsure, size down one from your regular size.
- **Slim Fit:** Closer to the body. If you prefer more room, size up one.
